# Competitive Intelligence Assistant

> **AI-powered competitive intelligence that monitors competitor websites and generates actionable PMM insights.**

Instead of manually checking competitor websites every week, receive an AI-generated competitive brief that explains **what changed, why it matters, and what your go-to-market team should do next.**

---

## The Problem

Product Marketing Managers spend hours every week monitoring competitor websites, product pages, blogs, pricing, and release notes.

Most website monitoring tools only tell you **that** something changed.

They don't explain:

- Why it matters
- How it impacts positioning
- Whether sales should care
- What action should be taken

---

## The Solution

Competitive Intelligence Assistant automatically:

- 🌐 Crawls competitor websites
- 🔍 Detects meaningful changes
- 🧠 Uses AI to interpret competitive impact
- 📄 Generates a weekly PMM brief
- ✅ Recommends actionable GTM responses

---

## Example Output

### Weekly Competitive Brief

**Competitor:** Adobe

### Observation

Adobe updated its homepage messaging to emphasize AI-powered creative workflows.

### Inference

This suggests Adobe is shifting its positioning toward AI-first productivity and enterprise buyers.

### Recommended Action

- Update enterprise battlecards
- Review homepage messaging
- Brief Sales before customer meetings
- Monitor pricing changes next week

## Why I Built This

Competitive intelligence is one of the core responsibilities of Product Marketing Managers.

This project explores how AI can automate repetitive competitive research while preserving the strategic thinking that PMMs bring to go-to-market decisions.

Rather than simply reporting website changes, the goal is to answer three questions:

1. **What changed?**
2. **Why does it matter?**
3. **What should we do?**
